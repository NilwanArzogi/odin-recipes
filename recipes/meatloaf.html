Recipes Meat Loaf

cook: 1 hr 15 mins
total: 1 hr 35 mins
Servings: 6

  • 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • ½ teaspoon freshly ground black pepper

  • 2 tablespoons brown sugar (for topping)
